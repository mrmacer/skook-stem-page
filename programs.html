Program 02

Make@Skook29

The Make@Skook29 makerspace is a hands-on innovation lab where students from across the region come to design, prototype, and build. It's learning by doing — and it changes how students think about what's possible.

Whether a student is building their first 3D model or engineering a functional prototype, the makerspace provides the tools, the guidance, and the space to take creative risks.

Program 03

CSO@Skook29

Career and Student Organizations are powerful, research-backed vehicles for career readiness and student development. STEM@Skook29 supports CSO programs across the region, connecting them to competition opportunities, resources, and industry partners.

Students who participate in CSO programs develop leadership, communication, teamwork, and industry-specific skills that employers and colleges are looking for.

Program 04

STEELS@Skook29

Pennsylvania's STEELS standards represent a major shift in how science, technology, engineering, environment, ecology, and sustainability are taught. That shift requires support — and that's what STEM@Skook29 delivers.

We provide teachers and administrators with the resources, training, and coaching they need to implement the STEELS framework with confidence — at every grade level and in every subject area.

EdTech & AI

Artificial intelligence is reshaping every field — and education is no exception. STEM@Skook29 helps teachers and students understand, use, and think critically about AI and emerging technologies.
